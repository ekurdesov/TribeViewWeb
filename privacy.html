Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 27, 2026 · Applies to all Stompai apps

Overview

This privacy policy applies to all applications published by Stompai, including but not limited to TribeView, BellaLingua (Google Play), and Vitality Clock (Garmin Connect IQ).

We believe your data is yours. Our apps are designed to function without collecting, storing, or transmitting your personal information to our servers. We do not maintain databases of user data and we do not sell, trade, or rent any information to third parties.

Information We Collect

Our apps use the following third-party services provided by Google Firebase:

Firebase Analytics

Collects anonymous usage data such as app opens, screen views, and general interaction patterns. This helps us understand how our apps are used so we can improve the experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Firebase Crashlytics

Automatically collects crash reports and diagnostic data when an error occurs. This includes device type, operating system version, and stack traces. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs.

Beyond these analytics services, we do not collect, store, or process any personal data. Your content, preferences, and activity remain on your device.

Data Storage

We do not operate servers that store user data. All app data is stored locally on your device. Analytics and crash data are processed by Firebase under Google's privacy terms.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party services integrated into our apps are:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics — Crashlytics Terms

We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle data.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect personal data from any users, there is no special handling required. If you believe a child has somehow provided personal information through our apps, please contact us so we can investigate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices, reach out to us at support@stompai.com.
